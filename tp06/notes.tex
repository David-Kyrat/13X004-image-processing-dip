Continuous Delta Function

\begin{equation*}
    \delta(t) = \begin{cases}
        \infty & t = 0 \\
        0 & t \neq 0
    \end{cases}
    \\ \int_{-\infty}^{\infty}{\delta(t)dt} = 1
\end{equation*}
